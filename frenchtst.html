French Toast

6 Brioche slices drizzled with maple syrup

This is our mouth-watering french toast. Made with french vanilla brioche, golden eggs, almond milk

Ingredients

  • Brioche Bread
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Cinnamon

Cooking Steps

  1. Mix all wet ingredients in a bowl&then add cinnamon.
  2. Butter pan at medium-heat.
  3. Dip bread in mixture coating both sides.
  4. Cook each side for 2 minutes or until golden crisp.
