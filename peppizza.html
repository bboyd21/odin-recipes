Pepperoni Pizza

This is our world famous pepperoni pizza. Made with our own aged tomato sauce, freshly shredded mozzerella cheese, and our delicious man-cut pepperoni

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Mozzerella
  • Pepperoni
  • Itailian Seasoning
  • Homemade pizza crust

Cooking Steps

  1. 1.Pre-heat oven to 450 degrees.
  2. 2.Spread your tomato sauce across the whole pizza in small circles.
  3. 3.As you are spreading your sauce, lightly season your sauce with italian seasoning.
  4. 4.Now sprinkle your mozzerella cheese overtop the sauce you just placed down.
  5. 5.Place an even amount of pepperoni slices on each half.
  6. 6.Final step, oil or butter the edges of the pizza crust and place the pizza into the oven for 8-10 minutes.
  7. Take Out Carefully.
  8. Enjoy!
